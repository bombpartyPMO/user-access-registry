⚙️ Governance & Policies

Mandatory access control policies for all BombParty personnel — Review Cycle: Quarterly

🔒 Policy Compliance — These policies are mandatory for all BombParty personnel. Non-compliance must be escalated to IT Security and HR. Violations may result in immediate access revocation and disciplinary action.
📋

Access Request Process

Standard workflow for all provisioning requests
  1. Requestor submits via the Access Requests tab in this registry.
  2. Direct manager is auto-notified for initial approval within 2 business days.
  3. System owner reviews and approves or denies within 2 business days.
  4. IT Security must co-approve all Admin-level and above requests.
  5. Provisioning is completed within 3 business days of all approvals.
  6. Access grant is logged in the registry and access matrix updated same-day.
🚪

Offboarding & Deprovisioning

SLA: 4 hours from official departure
✅ SLA: 4 Hours — All departing employee access must be revoked within 4 hours of official departure. HR submits the ticket; IT Security executes.
