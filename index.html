⚙️ Governance & Policies

Parent: System Access Registry
Review Cycle: Quarterly
🔒 Policy Compliance

These policies are mandatory for all BombParty personnel. Non-compliance must be escalated to IT Security and HR. Violations may result in immediate access revocation and disciplinary action.

📋 Access Request Process✎ Click to edit

  1. Requestor submits via the Access Requests page in this registry.
  2. Direct manager is auto-notified for initial approval within 2 business days.
  3. System owner reviews and approves or denies within 2 business days.
  4. IT Security must co-approve all Admin-level and above requests.
  5. Provisioning is completed within 3 business days of all approvals.
  6. Access grant is logged in the registry and access matrix updated same-day.

🚪 Offboarding & Deprovisioning✎ Click to edit

✅ SLA: 4 Hours

All departing employee access must be revoked within 4 hours of official departure. HR submits the ticket; IT Security executes.
